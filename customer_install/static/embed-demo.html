One-line install

Add this single line anywhere in your site's HTML (typically before </body>):

<script src="http://localhost:5051/static/embed.js" defer></script>

That's it. The chat button appears on every page of your site.

What your visitors get

1 One tap to chat. Customers click the floating button — no popups, no account creation, no waiting for a human. Orbi answers instantly, 24/7.
2 Voice or text. Toggle the mic to talk. Toggle the speaker for hands-free. Auto-mute prevents echo when Orbi speaks.
3 Add to home screen. Visitors can install Orbi as an app on their phone — no App Store needed. Your business icon shows up next to their other apps.
4 You get every lead. When a customer leaves their name and number, it's instantly in your owner dashboard. PWA push notification on your phone.
