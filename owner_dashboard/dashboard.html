📱 Add Orby to your iPhone

Important: Use Chrome (not Safari) on iPhone — Safari's home-screen install blocks Orby's voice. Chrome's shortcut works fully.

Step 1 — get Chrome if you don't have it:
Open the App Store, search "Google Chrome", install (free).

Step 2 — open this page in Chrome:

Copy the URL from the Settings → Install on Phone section, open Chrome, paste in the address bar, sign in.

Step 3 — add to home screen FROM Chrome:

  1. Tap the ⋯ menu (three dots, bottom-right of Chrome)
  2. Scroll down, tap Add to Home Screen
  3. Tap Add

✓ Orby icon appears on your home screen — tap it any time, voice and chat both work.

Why not Safari?

Apple restricts audio in installed Safari PWAs — Orby can't speak her replies reliably. Chrome's shortcut opens Orby as a browser tab (not a standalone PWA) which has full audio access. Same Orby, just opens in Chrome.

Step-by-step (iPhone & Android)
iPhone (Safari only):
  1. Scan the QR with your camera, tap the link
  2. Sign in with your email + password
  3. Tap Share (square with arrow up) → Add to Home Screen → Add
Android (Chrome / Edge):
  1. Scan the QR, tap the link
  2. Sign in
  3. Tap the Install Orby banner that pops up (or ⋮ menu → Install app)
